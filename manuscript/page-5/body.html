Priyanka Chopra, born in energetic, enthusiastic and straight-forward Aries ascendant, whose Lord Mars is placed in 6th House with Saturn. This makes her ambitious, mature and seeks herself to be a catalyst of change in the society.

A conjunction of Saturn and Mars makes her one-pointed focus and determined to succeed. She is an adept at analysing every little detail of any projects she takes on, giving her level best to make that absolutely perfect. Bharani, the Nakshatra lord of her Ascendent, again shows she has creative talents inherent in her.

Priyanka has Moon exalted in the sign of Taurus in Rohini Nakshatra. The Moon is placed in an Earthy sign of Taurus giving a strong and stable mind. Rohinis are excellent at dancing and singing, and are extremely appealing to look at. These are essential qualities for a successful actress.
